Our Mission

Three Pillars of Service

Shri Shankar Maharaj Seva Mandal, Chinchwad works across three core areas — elder care, child education, and environmental conservation — to build a better world.

Sneh Sawli Aaple Ghar

Our flagship old age home — a true family for abandoned and lonely elders. We provide 24/7 care, nutritious meals, health checkups, and a vibrant community full of warmth.

Education for All

We support underprivileged children with school fees, books, and mentoring — because every child deserves the chance to learn, dream, and grow regardless of their background.

Environmental Conservation

From blood donation camps to eco-friendly Ganpati murtis and scrap collection drives — we protect both lives and nature through community-driven initiatives.

Our Initiatives

How We Serve the Community

Every initiative under Shri Shankar Maharaj Seva Mandal, Chinchwad is powered by compassion and community generosity.

Elder Care

Sneh Sawli Aaple Ghar

Our flagship old age home provides a warm, dignified life for the elderly — with 24/7 care, nutritious meals, health checkups, yoga sessions, and a vibrant community of peers.

Health

Blood Donation Camps

We organize regular blood donation drives. Every drop you donate helps save lives in our community.

Education

Education for Poor Children

We fund school fees, provide books and supplies, and offer mentoring to underprivileged children — giving them the tools to dream, learn, and build a brighter future.

🌿 Eco-Friendly

Murti Aamchi, Kimmat Tumchi

During Ganpati festival, we sell natural shadu (sand) murtis — eco-friendly and biodegradable. You decide the price! All proceeds go directly to our welfare programs.

Save Nature

Scrap & Raddi Collection

We collect old newspapers, magazines, and scrap from apartments and housing societies. Recycling saves nature — and the funds raised support our elders and education programs.
